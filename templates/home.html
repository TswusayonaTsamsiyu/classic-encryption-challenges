Welcome young apprentices!

Before you stand 5 challenges, that will put your skills to the test!

You can interact with the API as follows:

/<challenge number>/encrypt/<plain text> to encrypt something.

/<challenge number>/hint if you need a hint.

The challenges are numbered 1-5 in no particular order.

Try to solve as many as you can!

Note that some encryption mechanisms also use a key!

Good luck!
